🍽️ Pappu Charu | Pappu Charu గారు recipe
(Telugu • Dal)

📅 Saturday, 04 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup toor dal
•  1 onion
•  1 tomato
•  2 green chillies
•  Small ball of tamarind
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tsp cumin
•  2 dry red chillies
•  Curry leaves
•  2 tbsp oil
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Pressure cook the toor dal with turmeric until soft and mash it lightly.
2. Boil onion, tomato and green chillies with tamarind water until soft.
3. Add the mashed dal, salt and simmer for ten minutes until it comes together.
4. Heat oil and make a tadka with mustard, cumin, red chillies and curry leaves and pour it on top.
5. Serve with hot rice and a dollop of ghee.
