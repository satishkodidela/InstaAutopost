🍽️ Pesarattu | Pesarattu recipe
(Telugu • Breakfast)

📅 Saturday, 04 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup whole green gram (pesalu), soaked overnight
•  2 green chillies
•  1 inch ginger
•  1 small onion, chopped fine
•  Few cumin seeds
•  Salt
•  Oil for roasting

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Grind the soaked green gram with green chillies, ginger, cumin and salt into a smooth batter with a little water.
2. Heat a tawa on medium heat and spread a ladle of batter thin like a dosa.
3. Sprinkle chopped onions and cumin on top and drizzle oil around the edges.
4. Roast until the bottom turns golden and crisp, then fold and serve hot.
5. Serve with ginger chutney (allam pachadi) for the classic combination.
