🍽️ Kodi Vepudu | కోడి వెదుడుడు recipe
(Telugu • Curry)

📅 Saturday, 04 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  500g chicken
•  2 onions, sliced
•  2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
•  2 tbsp red chilli powder
•  1 tsp garam masala
•  1 tsp black pepper
•  Curry leaves
•  4 tbsp oil
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Marinate the chicken with turmeric, salt, chilli powder and ginger garlic paste for 30 minutes.
2. Heat oil and cook the chicken covered on medium heat in its own juices.
3. Once the water evaporates, add sliced onions and fry on high heat.
4. Keep frying until the chicken turns dark, dry and crispy at the edges.
5. Finish with garam masala, black pepper and a handful of curry leaves.
