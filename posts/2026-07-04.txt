🍽️ Sakinalu | సకినాల్లు recipe
(Telugu • Snack)

📅 Saturday, 04 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  2 cups rice flour
•  2 tbsp sesame seeds
•  1 tsp ajwain (vamu)
•  Oil for deep frying
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Mix rice flour, sesame seeds, ajwain and salt with water into a smooth thick batter.
2. Heat oil in a wide kadai.
3. With your fingers, swirl the batter into the oil in circular spiral motions.
4. Fry until crisp and light golden on both sides.
5. Drain and store airtight — the classic Telangana Sankranti snack.
