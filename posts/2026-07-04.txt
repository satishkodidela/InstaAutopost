🍽️ Ragi Sangati | రాగి సంగాతి recipe
(Telugu • Main)

📅 Saturday, 04 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup ragi flour
•  half cup rice
•  4 cups water
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Cook the rice in water until very soft and mushy.
2. Lower the heat and sprinkle the ragi flour over the rice, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
3. Cook covered on low heat until the mixture thickens into a smooth dough.
4. Wet your hands and shape the sangati into round balls while warm.
5. Serve hot with natu kodi pulusu or any spicy gravy.
