🍽️ Gongura Pachadi | గోంగుర పచడీ recipe
(Telugu • Pickle)

📅 Sunday, 12 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  2 bunches gongura (sorrel leaves)
•  6 dry red chillies
•  2 tbsp oil
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tsp fenugreek seeds
•  4 garlic cloves
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Wash and dry the gongura leaves, then fry them in a little oil until they wilt completely.
2. In the same pan roast red chillies, fenugreek and mustard seeds until fragrant.
3. Grind the wilted leaves with the roasted spices, garlic and salt into a coarse paste.
4. Heat oil, add mustard seeds and garlic for tadka, and pour it over the pachadi.
5. Serve with hot rice and a spoon of ghee.
