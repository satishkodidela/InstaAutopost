🍽️ Natu Kodi Pulusu | నతు కోడి పులుసు recipe
(Telugu • Curry)

📅 Sunday, 12 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  500g country chicken
•  2 onions
•  2 tomatoes
•  2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
•  3 tbsp red chilli powder
•  1 tsp coriander powder
•  Small ball of tamarind
•  Curry leaves
•  4 tbsp oil
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Marinate the chicken with turmeric, salt and half the ginger garlic paste for 30 minutes.
2. Fry sliced onions in oil until deep golden, then add remaining ginger garlic paste and tomatoes.
3. Add the chicken, chilli powder, coriander powder and sear on high heat until coated.
4. Pour tamarind water, cover and simmer on low heat until the chicken is tender and the gravy thickens.
5. Finish with curry leaves and serve with ragi sangati or rice.
