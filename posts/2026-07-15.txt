🍽️ Bobbatlu | బాబ్‌బాట్‌లు recipe
(Telugu • Dessert)

📅 Wednesday, 15 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup chana dal
•  1 cup jaggery
•  1 cup maida
•  Pinch of cardamom powder
•  Ghee
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Cook the chana dal until just soft, drain fully and grind with jaggery and cardamom into a smooth purnam.
2. Knead maida with a pinch of salt, water and a spoon of ghee into a soft, elastic dough and rest for an hour.
3. Take a ball of dough, flatten it, and stuff a bigger ball of purnam inside and seal.
4. Roll it out gently into a thick circle on a greased sheet.
5. Roast on a tawa with ghee until golden spots appear on both sides. Serve warm.
