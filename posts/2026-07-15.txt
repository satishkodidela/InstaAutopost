🍽️ Dondakaya Vepudu | దోసకాయ బీఫ్ recipe
(Telugu • Curry)

📅 Wednesday, 15 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  250g ivy gourd (dondakaya)
•  1 onion
•  2 tbsp red chilli powder
•  1 tbsp peanut powder
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  Curry leaves
•  3 tbsp oil
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Slice the dondakaya lengthwise into thin strips.
2. Heat oil, crackle mustard seeds and add curry leaves and sliced onion.
3. Add the dondakaya with turmeric and salt, and fry uncovered on medium heat.
4. When they shrink and brown, sprinkle chilli powder and peanut powder.
5. Fry two more minutes and serve with rice.
