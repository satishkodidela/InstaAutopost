🍽️ Pulihora | పులిహోరా recipe
(Telugu • Rice)

📅 Tuesday, 07 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  3 cups cooked rice
•  1 lemon-sized ball of tamarind
•  3 green chillies
•  2 dry red chillies
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tsp chana dal
•  Curry leaves
•  2 tbsp peanuts
•  Pinch of turmeric
•  3 tbsp oil
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Soak the tamarind in warm water and extract a thick pulp.
2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, chana dal, peanuts, red and green chillies and curry leaves and fry until golden.
3. Add turmeric and the tamarind pulp and simmer until the raw smell goes and it thickens.
4. Mix this pulusu into the cooked rice gently with salt.
5. Rest for half an hour so the flavours soak in, then serve.
