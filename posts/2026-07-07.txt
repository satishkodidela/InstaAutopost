🍽️ Rava Dosa | రవ్వ దోశ recipe
(Telugu • Breakfast)

📅 Tuesday, 07 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup semolina (rava)
•  half cup rice flour
•  quarter cup maida
•  1 onion, chopped fine
•  2 green chillies
•  1 tsp cumin
•  Few curry leaves
•  Ginger, chopped
•  Salt
•  Oil

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Mix rava, rice flour, maida and salt with water into a very thin, watery batter and rest for 20 minutes.
2. Add chopped onion, chillies, ginger, cumin and curry leaves to the batter.
3. Heat the tawa well and pour the batter from a height in a circular motion to get a lacy dosa.
4. Drizzle oil around and roast until crisp and golden; do not flip.
5. Serve immediately with coconut chutney.
