🍽️ Sarva Pindi
సర్వపిండి recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  2 cups rice flour
•  1/2 cup chana dal (soaked 2 hours)
•  1 onion, finely chopped
•  4 green chillies, chopped
•  1 tbsp sesame seeds
•  1 sprig curry leaves, torn
•  1 tsp ginger garlic paste
•  1/4 cup peanuts (optional)
•  4 tbsp oil
•  salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Mix rice flour with soaked chana dal, onion, green chillies, sesame seeds, curry leaves, ginger garlic paste and salt.
2. Add water little by little and knead into a soft, spreadable dough.
3. Spread the dough by hand into a thick disc directly in a cold, oiled kadai and make holes with a finger.
4. Drizzle oil into the holes, cover and cook on medium heat until the bottom turns golden and crisp.
5. Flip carefully, cook the other side for a few minutes and serve hot.
