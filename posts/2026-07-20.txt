🍽️ Gongura Pappu
గోంగూర పప్పు recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup toor dal
•  2 cups gongura (sorrel) leaves, packed
•  1 onion, chopped
•  4 green chillies
•  1/2 tsp turmeric
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tsp cumin seeds
•  4 garlic cloves
•  2 dried red chillies
•  3 tbsp oil
•  salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Pressure cook toor dal with gongura leaves, onion, green chillies and turmeric.
2. Mash well so the tangy leaves blend into the dal; add salt and simmer.
3. Fry garlic, mustard, cumin and red chillies in hot oil until the garlic turns golden.
4. Pour the tempering over the pappu and cover for a minute so the aroma settles.
5. Serve with rice, ghee and a roasted papad.
