🍽️ Kakarakaya Vepudu | కక్కరకాయ వెదుడు recipe
(Telugu • Curry)

📅 Thursday, 16 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  250g bitter gourd (kakarakaya)
•  1 onion
•  2 tbsp red chilli powder
•  1 tbsp jaggery powder
•  1 tbsp peanut powder
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  Curry leaves
•  3 tbsp oil
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Slice the bitter gourd thin, rub with salt and turmeric and rest for 15 minutes, then squeeze out the juice.
2. Heat oil, crackle mustard seeds and fry the onion and curry leaves.
3. Add the bitter gourd slices and fry on medium heat until browned and crisp.
4. Sprinkle chilli powder, jaggery and peanut powder and toss.
5. Fry for two more minutes — bitterness gone, flavour on!
