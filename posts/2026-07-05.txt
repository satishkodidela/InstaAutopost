🍽️ Palakura Pappu recipe
(Telugu • Dal)

📅 Sunday, 05 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup toor dal
•  2 bunches spinach (palakura)
•  1 onion
•  2 green chillies
•  4 garlic cloves
•  Small ball of tamarind
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tsp cumin
•  Curry leaves
•  2 tbsp oil
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Pressure cook the dal with chopped spinach, onion, chillies and turmeric.
2. Mash lightly, add tamarind water and salt, and simmer for five minutes.
3. Make a tadka of mustard, cumin, garlic and curry leaves in oil.
4. Pour the tadka over the pappu.
5. Serve with hot rice and ghee.
