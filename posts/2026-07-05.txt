🍽️ Tomato Pappu | టమాటో పప్పూ recipe
(Telugu • Dal)

📅 Sunday, 05 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup toor dal
•  3 ripe tomatoes
•  2 green chillies
•  4 garlic cloves
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tsp cumin
•  2 dry red chillies
•  Curry leaves
•  2 tbsp ghee
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Pressure cook the dal with chopped tomatoes, green chillies and turmeric until soft.
2. Mash the dal and tomatoes together with salt, adding hot water for a pouring consistency.
3. Heat ghee and crackle mustard, cumin, garlic, red chillies and curry leaves.
4. Pour the tadka over the pappu and simmer for two minutes.
5. Serve with hot rice, ghee and a roasted papad.
