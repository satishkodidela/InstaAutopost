🍽️ Gongura Mutton | గోగుర మటన్ recipe
(Telugu • Curry)

📅 Sunday, 05 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  500g mutton
•  2 bunches gongura leaves
•  2 onions
•  2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
•  3 tbsp red chilli powder
•  1 tsp garam masala
•  4 tbsp oil
•  Curry leaves
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Pressure cook the mutton with turmeric, salt and half the ginger garlic paste until tender.
2. Fry the gongura leaves separately until wilted and mash them.
3. Fry onions in oil until golden, add remaining paste, chilli powder and the cooked mutton.
4. Add the mashed gongura and simmer until the gravy coats the mutton and oil separates.
5. Finish with garam masala and curry leaves. Serve with rice.
