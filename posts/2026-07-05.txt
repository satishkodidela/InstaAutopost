🍽️ Chepala Pulusu | చెపలా పులుసు recipe
(Telugu • Curry)

📅 Sunday, 05 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  500g fish pieces
•  2 onions
•  2 tomatoes
•  Big ball of tamarind
•  3 tbsp red chilli powder
•  1 tbsp coriander powder
•  2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
•  Curry leaves
•  4 tbsp oil
•  Fenugreek seeds
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Fry onions with fenugreek seeds until golden, then add ginger garlic paste and tomatoes.
2. Add chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric and thick tamarind juice and boil.
3. Slide the fish pieces in gently and do not stir — just swirl the pan.
4. Simmer on low heat for 20 minutes until the oil floats on top.
5. Rest for a few hours before serving — pulusu tastes best the next day!
