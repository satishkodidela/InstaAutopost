🍽️ Bendakaya Pulusu | బెండకాయ పులుసు recipe
(Telugu • Curry)

📅 Sunday, 05 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  250g okra (bendakaya)
•  2 onions
•  1 tomato
•  Big ball of tamarind
•  2 tbsp red chilli powder
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  Fenugreek seeds
•  Curry leaves
•  3 tbsp oil
•  Jaggery, small piece
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Fry the okra pieces until the sliminess disappears and set aside.
2. In the same oil, crackle mustard and fenugreek, then fry onions and tomato.
3. Add tamarind juice, chilli powder, turmeric, salt and jaggery and boil.
4. Add the fried okra and simmer until the pulusu thickens.
5. Serve with hot rice — sweet, sour and spicy in one bowl.
