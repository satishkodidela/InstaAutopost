🍽️ Chintapandu Rasam | చిత్తాపుండూ రసం recipe
(Telugu • Soup)

📅 Wednesday, 08 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  Small ball of tamarind
•  1 tomato
•  1 tsp rasam powder
•  half tsp black pepper
•  1 tsp cumin
•  4 garlic cloves
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  2 dry red chillies
•  Curry leaves
•  Coriander leaves
•  1 tbsp ghee
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Soak tamarind and extract thin juice; crush the tomato into it.
2. Add rasam powder, turmeric, salt and crushed garlic, pepper and cumin.
3. Simmer gently until frothy on top — do not boil hard.
4. Make a ghee tadka of mustard, red chillies and curry leaves and pour over.
5. Garnish with coriander; drink hot or mix with rice.
