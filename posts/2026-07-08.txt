🍽️ Perugu Annam (Curd Rice) | పెరుగూ అన్నం (Curd Rice) recipe
(Telugu • Rice)

📅 Wednesday, 08 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  2 cups cooked rice
•  1 cup thick curd
•  half cup milk
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tbsp chana dal
•  2 green chillies
•  1 inch ginger
•  Curry leaves
•  Pomegranate pearls
•  2 tbsp oil
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Mash the warm rice slightly and mix in curd, milk and salt.
2. Heat oil and crackle mustard seeds, chana dal, chillies, ginger and curry leaves.
3. Mix the tadka into the rice.
4. Top with pomegranate pearls.
5. Serve chilled — the perfect summer comfort.
