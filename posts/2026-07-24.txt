🍽️ Mirapakaya Bajji
మిరపకాయ బజ్జి recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  8 large bajji chillies
•  2 cups besan (gram flour)
•  2 tbsp rice flour
•  1/2 tsp carom seeds (vaamu)
•  1/2 tsp red chilli powder
•  1 pinch baking soda
•  1 onion, finely chopped
•  1 lemon
•  2 tbsp coriander leaves
•  oil for deep frying
•  salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Slit the bajji chillies, remove the seeds, and rub a little salt inside.
2. Whisk besan, rice flour, carom seeds, chilli powder, soda and salt with water into a thick coating batter.
3. Dip each chilli in the batter and deep fry in hot oil until golden and puffed.
4. Drain, split open, and stuff with chopped onion, coriander and a squeeze of lemon.
5. Serve hot — the classic rainy-day roadside bajji.
