🍽️ Gutti Vankaya Kura | గుత్తు వంగా కూర్లా recipe
(Telugu • Curry)

📅 Monday, 06 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  8 small brinjals
•  3 tbsp peanuts
•  2 tbsp sesame seeds
•  1 tbsp coriander seeds
•  4 dry red chillies
•  1 onion
•  Small ball of tamarind
•  3 tbsp oil
•  Curry leaves
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Dry roast peanuts, sesame, coriander seeds and red chillies, then grind with tamarind and salt into a stuffing masala.
2. Slit the brinjals crosswise keeping the stem intact, and stuff them generously with the masala.
3. Heat oil in a kadai, place the stuffed brinjals and fry gently on low heat, turning carefully.
4. Add the remaining masala with a little water, cover and simmer until the brinjals are soft.
5. Finish with curry leaves and serve with rice or chapati.
