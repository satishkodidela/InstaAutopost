🍽️ Mysore Bonda | మైసూరు బొండ recipe
(Telugu • Snack)

📅 Monday, 06 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup maida
•  half cup curd
•  1 tsp cumin
•  1 inch ginger, chopped
•  2 green chillies
•  Few curry leaves
•  Pinch of baking soda
•  Oil for frying
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Mix maida, curd, salt and soda into a thick batter and rest for two hours.
2. Add cumin, ginger, chillies and curry leaves to the batter.
3. Heat oil on medium flame.
4. Wet your hand, take a ball of batter and drop gently into the oil.
5. Fry until puffed and golden. Serve with coconut chutney.
