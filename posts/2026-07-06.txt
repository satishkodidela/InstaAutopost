🍽️ Garelu (Medu Vada) | గారూ (మెడువాడ) recipe
(Telugu • Snack)

📅 Monday, 06 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup urad dal
•  2 green chillies
•  1 inch ginger
•  Few curry leaves
•  1 tsp black pepper
•  Oil for frying
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Soak the urad dal for four hours and grind into a thick, fluffy batter with minimal water.
2. Mix in chopped chillies, ginger, curry leaves, pepper and salt.
3. Wet your hands, shape a ball, flatten and make a hole in the center.
4. Slide into hot oil and fry until golden and crisp.
5. Serve hot with chutney — a must for every festival!
