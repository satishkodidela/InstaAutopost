🍽️ Semiya Payasam | సెమియా పేసమ్ recipe
(Telugu • Dessert)

📅 Tuesday, 14 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup vermicelli (semiya)
•  4 cups milk
•  half cup sugar
•  10 cashews
•  10 raisins
•  Pinch of cardamom powder
•  2 tbsp ghee

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Fry cashews and raisins in ghee until golden and set aside.
2. In the same ghee, roast the vermicelli until light brown.
3. Boil the milk, add the vermicelli and cook until soft.
4. Add sugar and cardamom and simmer until slightly thick.
5. Top with fried cashews and raisins. Serve warm or chilled.
