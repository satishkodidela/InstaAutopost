🍽️ Kandi Pachadi
కంది పచ్చడి recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup toor dal (kandi pappu)
•  6 dried red chillies
•  1 tsp cumin seeds
•  3 garlic cloves
•  1 small ball tamarind
•  2 tbsp oil
•  salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Dry roast the toor dal on low heat until golden and nutty; set aside.
2. Roast the red chillies and cumin in a spoon of oil.
3. Grind the dal coarsely first, then add chillies, cumin, garlic, tamarind and salt.
4. Grind again with a splash of water to a thick, coarse pachadi.
5. Serve with hot rice and a big spoon of ghee — Telangana's protein-packed everyday chutney.
