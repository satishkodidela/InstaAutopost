కొబ్బరి పచ్చడి recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup fresh grated coconut
•  2 green chillies
•  1 inch ginger
•  2 tbsp roasted chana dal
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 dry red chilli
•  Curry leaves
•  1 tbsp oil
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Grind coconut, green chillies, ginger, roasted chana dal and salt with a little water into a smooth chutney.
2. Heat oil and crackle mustard seeds, red chilli and curry leaves.
3. Pour the tadka over the chutney and mix.
4. Serve with dosa, idli or pesarattu.
