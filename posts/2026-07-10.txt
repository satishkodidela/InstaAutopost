🍽️ Idli | ఇడ్లీ recipe
(Telugu • Breakfast)

📅 Friday, 10 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  2 cups idli rice
•  1 cup urad dal
•  1 tsp fenugreek seeds
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Soak rice, and dal with fenugreek, separately for six hours.
2. Grind the dal into a fluffy batter, then the rice, and mix both with salt.
3. Ferment overnight in a warm place until doubled.
4. Pour into greased idli plates.
5. Steam for 12 minutes; rest 2 minutes before unmoulding. Serve with sambar and chutney.
