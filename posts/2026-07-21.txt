🍽️ Dosakaya Pappu
దోసకాయ పప్పు recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup toor dal
•  2 cups dosakaya (yellow cucumber), cubed
•  2 tomatoes, chopped
•  4 green chillies
•  1 small ball tamarind
•  1/2 tsp turmeric
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tsp cumin seeds
•  3 garlic cloves, crushed
•  2 dried red chillies
•  1 sprig curry leaves
•  3 tbsp oil
•  salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Pressure cook toor dal with dosakaya cubes, tomato, green chillies and turmeric until soft.
2. Mash lightly, add tamarind water and salt, and simmer for five minutes.
3. Heat oil and crackle mustard, cumin, garlic, red chillies and curry leaves.
4. Pour the tempering over the simmering pappu and mix once.
5. Serve hot with rice and a spoon of ghee.
