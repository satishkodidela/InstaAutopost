🍽️ Sambar | సాంబార్ recipe
(Telugu • Dal)

📅 Thursday, 09 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup toor dal
•  1 drumstick
•  1 carrot
•  5 shallots
•  1 tomato
•  Small ball of tamarind
•  2 tbsp sambar powder
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  2 dry red chillies
•  Curry leaves
•  2 tbsp oil
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Pressure cook the dal with turmeric until soft and mash it.
2. Boil the vegetables with tamarind water, salt and sambar powder until tender.
3. Add the mashed dal and simmer for ten minutes.
4. Make a tadka of mustard, red chillies and curry leaves in oil and pour over.
5. Serve with rice, idli or dosa.
