🍽️ Aratikaya Vepudu | అరటిపండ్ల ఈటర్ recipe
(Telugu • Curry)

📅 Saturday, 11 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  3 raw bananas
•  1 tbsp red chilli powder
•  1 tsp coriander powder
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tbsp chana dal
•  Curry leaves
•  3 tbsp oil
•  Turmeric
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Peel and cube the raw bananas and soak in turmeric water.
2. Heat oil, crackle mustard seeds and chana dal, then add curry leaves.
3. Add the drained banana cubes and fry on medium heat until they soften.
4. Sprinkle chilli powder, coriander powder and salt.
5. Fry until the edges crisp up. Serve with rice and pappu.
