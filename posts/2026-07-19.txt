🍽️ Mamidikaya Pappu
మామిడికాయ పప్పు recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup toor dal
•  1 raw mango, peeled and cubed
•  4 green chillies
•  1/2 tsp turmeric
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tsp cumin seeds
•  4 garlic cloves, crushed
•  2 dried red chillies
•  1 sprig curry leaves
•  3 tbsp oil
•  salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Pressure cook toor dal with raw mango pieces, green chillies and turmeric until soft.
2. Mash to a coarse consistency, add salt and simmer briefly — the mango gives all the sourness needed.
3. Crackle mustard, cumin, garlic, red chillies and curry leaves in hot oil.
4. Pour the sizzling tempering over the pappu.
5. Serve with hot rice and ghee — the classic summer dal of Telugu homes.
