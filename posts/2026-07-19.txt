🍽️ Avakaya — ముక్క మెత్తబడకుండా ఉండే పర్ఫెక్ట్ సీక్రెట్
ఆవకాయ recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  4 raw mangoes, hard and sour
•  1 cup red chilli powder
•  3/4 cup mustard powder
•  3/4 cup salt
•  1 tbsp fenugreek seeds
•  1 cup sesame oil
•  8 garlic cloves (optional)

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Wipe the mangoes completely dry and cut into cubes with the hard shell intact.
2. Mix red chilli powder, mustard powder, salt and fenugreek seeds in a dry bowl.
3. Toss the mango pieces and garlic in the masala until every piece is coated thick.
4. Pack into a dry ceramic jar and pour sesame oil until the masala is soaked through.
5. Rest three days, mix once with a dry spoon, top with more oil, and it is ready — the king of Telugu pickles.
