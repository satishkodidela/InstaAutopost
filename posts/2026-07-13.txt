🍽️ Upma | ఉప్మా recipe
(Telugu • Breakfast)

📅 Monday, 13 July 2026

🛒 Ingredients:
•  1 cup semolina (rava)
•  1 onion
•  2 green chillies
•  1 inch ginger
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 tbsp chana dal
•  Few cashews
•  Curry leaves
•  3 tbsp ghee
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Dry roast the rava until fragrant and set aside.
2. Heat ghee, crackle mustard seeds, then fry chana dal, cashews, onion, chillies, ginger and curry leaves.
3. Add two and a half cups of water with salt and bring to a rolling boil.
4. Lower the flame and pour the rava in slowly, stirring continuously.
5. Cover and steam on low heat for three minutes; fluff and serve hot.
