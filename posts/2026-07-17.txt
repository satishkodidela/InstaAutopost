🍽️ Punugulu
పునుగులు recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  2 cups leftover idli or dosa batter
•  1 onion, chopped fine
•  2 green chillies
•  Few curry leaves
•  1 tbsp rice flour
•  Oil for deep frying
•  Salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Mix chopped onion, green chillies, curry leaves, rice flour and salt into the batter to make it thick.
2. Heat oil in a kadai on medium flame.
3. Drop small spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil.
4. Fry until golden brown and crisp on all sides.
5. Drain and serve hot with ginger chutney.
