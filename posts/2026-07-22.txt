🍽️ Allam Pachadi
అల్లం పచ్చడి recipe

🔖 Save cheyandi — recipe mottham caption lo!

🛒 Ingredients:
•  100g fresh ginger, peeled
•  1 lemon-sized ball tamarind
•  1/2 cup jaggery, grated
•  6 dried red chillies
•  1 tbsp urad dal
•  1 tsp mustard seeds
•  1 sprig curry leaves
•  4 tbsp oil
•  salt

👨‍🍳 Method:
1. Fry the ginger pieces in a little oil until the raw smell goes.
2. Fry urad dal and red chillies until golden.
3. Grind ginger, dal, chillies, tamarind, jaggery and salt into a thick sweet-hot-sour paste.
4. Temper mustard seeds and curry leaves in oil, and mix into the pachadi.
5. Serve with pesarattu, idli or hot rice — it keeps for a week refrigerated.
